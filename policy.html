Progress Refunds Policy

Refund & Reward Policy

Accuracy-based · 基于出餐准确率
New staff not working alone are not included in the calculation · 新员工不独立站岗的不纳入计算

Penalty 罚款规则

No penalty if refund rate stays under 2% of shift sales
退单率低于当班营业额2%不触发罚款
If refund rate goes over 2%, the responsible staff from that shift will cover 50% of the excess refund amount
超出2%的部分，由当班责任人赔偿超出金额的50%
If 2 staff are working the same shift, both staff will share 50% of the excess refund amount
两人同时在站，共同承担超出部分的50%
Refunds are counted by individual shift only, not shared across different shifts
按个人班次单独计算，不跨班次分摊

Reward 奖励规则

Each shift with refund rate under 2% gets 1 point
每个退单率低于2%的班次积1分
Collect 7 points → get 2 hours bonus pay
累积满7分 → 奖励2小时工时
If refund rate goes over 2%, no point for that shift, but existing points stay
超标班次不积分，但已有积分保留
Points keep rolling over until redeemed
积分持续累积直到兑换
