Spicy Soup Base

Ingredients
  • Haidilao Beef Tallow Spicy Seasoning1 pack (480g)
  • Spicy Flavour Hot Pot Soup Base1 pack (400g)
  • Hot Pot Seasoning1 pack (22g)
  • Pork Bone Paste50g
  • Water2400 ml
Vessel
Stainless Steel Pot
Steps
  • 1Make pork bone paste first (see card below).
  • 2Measure 2400ml water into the pot.
  • 3Add all packs and the pork bone paste.
  • 4Heat on induction stove.
  • 5Stir often, don't let it stick.
Core Temperature≥ 75°C
Shelf Life12 hours

Pork Bone Paste — for Spicy Soup Base

Ingredients
  • Tonkotsu Ramen Soup 1610 (White Pork Soup)1 pack (1kg)
  • Water400 ml
Steps
  • 1Mix the pork soup pack with water.
  • 2Stir until smooth.
  • 3Use 50g of this paste in the Spicy Soup Base.
Note: Sub-recipe for Spicy Soup Base only. Not served on its own.

Spicy Sesame Dry Mix

Ingredients
  • Peanut and Sesame Sauce1 kg
  • Water1000 ml
Vessel
Stainless Steel Pot
Steps
  • 1Open the sauce, pour into a clean container.
  • 2Add water and mix until smooth, no lumps.
Seasoning — add after mixing
  • Sesame Oil1 full hot pot ladle
  • Chilli Dry Mix1 white spoon
  • Chilli Oil1 full hot pot ladle
  • Pepper Oil2 pumps
  • 3Add sesame oil, chilli dry mix, chilli oil, and pepper oil.
  • 4Mix well before serving.
Room Temp Storage12 hours
Fridge Storage24 hours
